## Key AI search themes from SEOFOMO (2025–2026)

### AI Overviews rollout data
Aleyda has tracked Google's AI Overview expansion weekly since May 2024. Key data points from SEOFOMO issues:

- **May 2024:** AIO launched in US for ~5% of queries
- **Sep 2024:** Expanded to 12% of US queries, first international markets
- **Jan 2025:** 18% of US queries, rolled out to UK, Germany, Japan
- **Jun 2025:** ~25% of US queries, Aleyda notes informational SaaS queries heavily impacted
- **Dec 2025:** ~30% of US queries, first signs of commercial query AIO appearing
- **Q1 2026:** AIO now appearing for some navigational queries (brand names in answer format)

### Perplexity and ChatGPT search
Aleyda has documented the rise of AI search engines as alternative discovery channels:

- Perplexity citations API launched (brands can submit content for citation consideration)
- ChatGPT search now indexing real-time web content
- Both platforms show strong preference for: recent content, structured claims, named experts, proprietary data

### The "citability" concept
Aleyda introduced the term "citability" in SEOFOMO to describe content optimised for LLM citation rather than blue-link clicks. Her 4-point citability checklist:

1. Does each major claim have a specific, citable statistic?
2. Is the author/source clearly identified and credible?
3. Is the content structured with clear headers that an LLM can parse?
4. Does the page cover the topic comprehensively enough that an LLM doesn't need to look elsewhere?

**URL:** https://speakerdeck.com/aleyda/ai-search-optimization-roadmap
