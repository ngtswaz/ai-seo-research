The metric most B2B SaaS content teams track: traffic.
The metric that actually matters: pipeline from organic.

I've worked with 200+ SaaS companies on content strategy. The ones with the most traffic aren't always the ones with the most pipeline.

The ones with the most pipeline all do one thing differently: they write content that matches buyer intent at every stage, not just top-of-funnel.

With AI, teams are tempted to go wide — publish on every keyword, fill every gap. Wrong move.

Go deep on the 20 topics your buyers actually care about. Use AI to make those 20 pieces the most comprehensive resources on the internet on those topics.

20 deep > 200 shallow. Every time.
