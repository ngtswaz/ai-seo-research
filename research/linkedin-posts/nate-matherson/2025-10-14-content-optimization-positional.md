The content teams winning in B2B SaaS right now have one thing most don't: a feedback loop.

Publish → measure → update → measure again.

Most teams do the first two. Almost none do the third and fourth systematically.

At Positional we track what happens after content is updated. The data is pretty clear:

- Content updated within 6 months of publishing: +38% avg traffic gain
- Content updated after 12 months: +19% avg traffic gain
- Content never updated: steady decay, -3 to -8% per month after month 6

The freshness signal matters. But more importantly, updating forces you to improve the piece — add new data, answer questions that appeared in Search Console, fix gaps.

AI makes this faster. You can draft an update in 10 minutes that would have taken 3 hours.

The teams using AI for updates, not just new content, are the ones pulling ahead.
