I ran 14 SEO experiments in 2025. Here's what actually moved the needle:

❌ Didn't work:
- Publishing more frequently (diminishing returns after ~2x/week)
- Longer articles (no correlation with rankings above 1,500 words)
- Exact-match anchor text in internal links

✅ Did work:
- Updating old content with new data: +40% traffic avg on refreshed posts
- Adding comparison tables to informational content: +22% CTR
- Removing thin pages from index: +15% site-wide crawl efficiency

The biggest unlock in 2025: treating SEO like a product team treats features. Hypothesis → test → measure → iterate.

Most SEO teams still run on intuition. The ones running on experiments are pulling ahead fast.
