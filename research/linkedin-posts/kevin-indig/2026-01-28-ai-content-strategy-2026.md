The AI content question in 2026 isn't "do we use it?" It's "how do we operationalize it without losing our brand voice?"

Three models I'm seeing work in B2B SaaS:

**Model 1: AI for research, humans for writing**
AI scrapes SERPs, summarizes competitors, identifies gaps. Human writes from that brief. Output quality is high but slow.

**Model 2: AI for drafts, humans for editing**
AI drafts, human rewrites the intro, adds proprietary data, tightens the conclusion. 3x faster than model 1, 80% of the quality.

**Model 3: AI for everything, QA gate before publish**
Full AI draft → automated quality check → human approves or rejects. Scales to hundreds of pieces/month. Quality variance is high.

Most B2B SaaS teams should be in Model 2. Model 3 only works if you've solved quality measurement first.
