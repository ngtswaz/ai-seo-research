We've scored over 2 million pieces of content in Surfer. Here are the benchmarks that actually correlate with rankings.

Content Score by position:
- Position 1: avg score 83/100
- Position 3: avg score 74/100
- Position 10: avg score 61/100
- Not ranking: avg score 48/100

The gap between "ranking" and "not ranking" is about 13 points.
The gap between "ranking" and "position 1" is about 22 points.

What moves score the most:
1. Adding missing NLP terms (avg +8 points)
2. Improving heading structure to cover subtopics (+6 points)
3. Increasing content length to match top performers (+5 points)
4. Adding internal links to related content (+4 points)

AI content that scores above 70 on publish day has a 3x higher chance of reaching page 1 within 90 days.
