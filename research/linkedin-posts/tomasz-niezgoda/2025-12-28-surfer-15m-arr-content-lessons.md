Surfer hit $15M ARR. Here's what content did and didn't do to get us there.

What content did:
- Built organic from 0 to 200k monthly visitors in 3 years
- Established Surfer as the authority on content optimization (not just a tool)
- Drove 40% of new trial signups at peak

What content didn't do:
- It didn't convert well without a strong free trial
- It didn't retain users who hadn't understood the core use case
- It couldn't fix a product that users found confusing

The lesson: content multiplies whatever's already working. If distribution is broken, more content makes it worse. If product-market fit is soft, more content is noise.

In 2025 with AI, you can produce 10x the content. The question is whether you've earned the right to scale.
