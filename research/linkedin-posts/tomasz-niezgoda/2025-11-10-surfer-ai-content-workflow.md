When we built Surfer AI, the hardest product decision wasn't the AI.

It was figuring out where to put the human in the loop.

Option A: Human at the start (brief only), AI writes everything
Option B: Human reviews AI draft section by section
Option C: Human edits fully-written AI output

We tested all three with 200 customers. The results surprised us.

Option A produced the most consistent output but lowest quality ceiling.
Option C produced the highest quality peaks but most variance.
Option B was the clear winner on average quality at consistent speed.

The insight: humans edit better when they're reviewing smaller chunks. A 2,000-word AI draft overwhelms the editorial eye. Four 500-word sections don't.

We redesigned the product around Option B. Content quality scores went up 31%.
