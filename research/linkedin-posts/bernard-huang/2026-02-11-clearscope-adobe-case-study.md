One of our enterprise customers (a Fortune 500 software company) ran a controlled experiment last quarter.

Team A: wrote content without Clearscope, relied on writer instinct
Team B: used Clearscope grades on every piece before publishing

After 90 days:
- Team B's content ranked on page 1 at 2.3x the rate of Team A
- Time to first ranking: Team B averaged 34 days vs 61 days for Team A
- Content update lift: Team B's rewrites of underperforming content saw +67% traffic avg

The variable wasn't talent. Both teams had good writers.
The variable was systematic topic coverage vs guesswork.

This is why I built Clearscope. Not to replace writers. To give them a map.
