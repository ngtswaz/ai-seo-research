We analyzed 50,000 articles published in 2025. Half written by humans, half AI-assisted.

The ranking difference wasn't about who wrote them. It was about topic coverage.

AI articles that ranked well had one thing in common: a human edited them for depth, not just grammar.

The AI would write 1,500 words hitting the main keyword. The human would add:
- The edge cases the AI glossed over
- The "but what about..." questions readers actually have
- One piece of data or example that couldn't be found by scraping the web

That's the delta. Not length. Not keyword density. Depth of coverage on the things that matter.

At Clearscope, we call this Content Grade. It's the single metric that correlates most strongly with ranking performance across our customer base.

Grade your content. Don't just generate it.
