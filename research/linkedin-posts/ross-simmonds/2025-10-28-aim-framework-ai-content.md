Most brands use AI to create more content.
The smart ones use AI to distribute existing content better.

I call it the AIM Framework: AI for Ideation, for Implementation, for Measurement.

Most people only use the middle one (Implementation = writing).

The real leverage is in Ideation and Measurement.

**AI for Ideation:**
- Analyze top-performing content across your category
- Identify gaps in competitor content
- Generate 50 angle variations on your best-performing post
- Find underserved questions your audience is searching

**AI for Measurement:**
- Classify what content types drive what outcomes
- Predict which topics will trend based on search data
- Identify which posts are decaying and need updating

The brands using all three legs of AIM are producing 4x the output with the same team size.

Distribution is still the moat. AI just lets you build it faster.
