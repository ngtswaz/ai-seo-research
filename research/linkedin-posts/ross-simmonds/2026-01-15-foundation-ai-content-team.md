We restructured our content team around AI 14 months ago. Here's what the org looks like now vs before.

**Before:**
- 6 writers
- 1 editor
- 1 SEO specialist
- 1 project manager
Total: 9 people, ~30 pieces/month

**After:**
- 2 senior writers (strategy + quality gatekeeping)
- 1 AI content engineer (prompts, workflows, QA systems)
- 1 distribution specialist
- 1 data analyst (tracks what content drives pipeline)
Total: 5 people, ~120 pieces/month

Output: 4x
Quality (by engagement and conversion): up
Cost: down 40%

The role that didn't exist before: AI Content Engineer. This person builds the systems the writers work inside. It's the highest-leverage hire we've made.
