Create once. Distribute forever.

That was my mantra before AI. Now the mantra is:

Create once. Distribute forever. At scale.

Here's what "at scale" looks like with AI in our workflow at Foundation:

One research report becomes:
→ 12 LinkedIn posts (one insight per post)
→ 3 email newsletter segments
→ 8 Twitter/X threads
→ 2 YouTube scripts
→ 5 podcast talking points
→ 4 blog posts (different angles for different audiences)

Time to create the original report: 40 hours
Time to repurpose everything with AI: 6 hours

The content team that used to ship 2 pieces/week is now shipping 20 touchpoints from the same research.

The moat isn't creating more. It's distributing smarter.
