Hot take: E-E-A-T is the most important thing in SEO right now and almost nobody is implementing it correctly.

Most brands treat it as a checklist:
✅ Add author bio
✅ Link to LinkedIn
✅ Call it done

That's not E-E-A-T. That's theater.

Real experience signals look like:
- Specific numbers from your own data ("we tested 400 articles...")
- Named clients or case studies with actual outcomes
- First-person accounts of failure, not just success
- Opinions that contradict conventional wisdom (and can back it up)

AI can write around a topic forever. It cannot write FROM experience.

That gap is your competitive advantage in 2026. The brands that fill their content with genuine practitioner voice will outperform the ones scaling generic AI output by 10x.
