AI Overviews are now showing for ~15% of all queries in the US.

I've been tracking this across 30+ client accounts since May 2024. Here's what the data actually shows:

Sites losing traffic to AIO:
- Informational queries: -18% to -34% CTR on average
- Top-of-funnel "what is" and "how does" content hit hardest
- Position 1 no longer means what it used to

Sites holding or gaining:
- Strong brand signals (E-E-A-T really matters now)
- Proprietary data, original research, first-hand experience
- Content that gets *cited* inside the AIO itself

The brands getting cited in AI Overviews aren't the ones with the most backlinks. They're the ones with the clearest expertise signals.

Stop optimizing for ranking. Start optimizing to be cited.
