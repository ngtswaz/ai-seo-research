The most contrarian take I can give you on AI content:

Most B2B SaaS companies should produce LESS content, not more.

I've consulted for 50+ SaaS companies. The ones with the best organic growth rarely have the most content. They have the most *useful* content.

AI has made it trivially easy to publish 50 articles a month. So now everyone is. Which means:

1. SERPs are flooded with nearly-identical AI content
2. The bar to stand out has gone up, not down
3. Google's quality filters are working overtime

The companies winning in 2025:
- Publish 4-6 deeply differentiated pieces per month
- Each one is genuinely difficult to replicate
- Every piece is tied to a product use case, not just a keyword

Keyword-led content strategy made sense in 2018. Product-led content strategy is what 2025 requires.
