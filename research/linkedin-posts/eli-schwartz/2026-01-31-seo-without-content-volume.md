Unpopular opinion: the best SEO strategy for most B2B SaaS companies has nothing to do with blogging.

It's building product features that create their own indexable pages.

Examples:
- Free tool that generates a shareable result page
- Public directory of integrations, templates, or use cases
- User-generated content that lives on your domain

These scale without a content team. They accrue backlinks naturally. They convert at higher rates because they're already in the product.

I've watched companies kill their blogs and triple organic traffic by investing in product-led indexable assets instead.

The companies that understand this use AI to build better product features that generate content, not to write more blog posts.

That's the highest-leverage version of AI + SEO in 2026.
