I've been calling it AISO: AI Search Optimization.

It's not a rebrand of SEO. It's a new layer on top of it.

Here's the framework I've been using with clients:

**Layer 1 - Traditional SEO foundation** (still required)
Crawlability, indexation, page speed, backlinks. None of this goes away. It's table stakes.

**Layer 2 - Content comprehensiveness** (amplified by AI)
Covering topics completely, not just targeting keywords. AI search rewards depth.

**Layer 3 - Citation optimization** (new)
Structuring content so LLMs can extract and cite specific claims. Think: clear definitions, named methodologies, data with attribution.

**Layer 4 - Brand mention monitoring** (new)
Are you being mentioned in AI answers? For what queries? With what framing? You need to track this now.

Most brands are stuck at Layer 1. The ones pulling ahead are at Layer 3.
