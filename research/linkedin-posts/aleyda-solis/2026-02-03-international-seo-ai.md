AI translation for international SEO: the good, the bad, and the actually dangerous.

I've helped 40+ B2B SaaS companies expand internationally. The ones using AI translation naively are making mistakes that take 12-18 months to recover from.

**What AI translation does well:**
- Speed (obviously)
- Basic accuracy across major languages
- Structural consistency

**What it consistently gets wrong:**
- Local search intent (Spanish speakers in Spain vs Mexico search differently)
- Industry terminology that varies by market
- Cultural nuance that affects trust signals

**The dangerous mistake:**
Publishing AI-translated content with hreflang implementation errors. Google then serves the wrong language version to the wrong market, and you lose rankings in both.

Minimum viable process: AI draft → native speaker review → hreflang audit before launch.
