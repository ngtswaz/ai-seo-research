We analyzed 900,000 AI-generated articles. Here's what the data shows about which ones rank.

(This took 6 months. Thread incoming.)

The short version:

AI articles that rank have one thing in common: they were written about topics where the AI had good training data. Niche, emerging, or highly technical topics? AI underperforms badly.

AI articles that don't rank:
- Published on topics with few quality sources (AI hallucinates to fill gaps)
- No original data or proprietary insight
- Published and never touched again

AI articles that do rank:
- Topics with abundant, high-quality source material
- At least one human-added data point or example not available online
- Updated within 90 days of publishing based on Search Console data

The "AI content doesn't rank" crowd and the "AI content ranks fine" crowd are both right — for different types of content.

Knowing which is which is the actual skill.
