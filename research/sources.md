## Why these 10 and not others

These experts were selected over the "first Google results" names (agencies, tools, listicles) on three criteria:

**1. They ship, not just advise.**
Bernard Huang built Clearscope. Tomasz Niezgoda built Surfer. Ross Simmonds runs Foundation. Ryan Law runs Ahrefs' content. Their opinions are grounded in systems that process real AI content at scale.

**2. They have documented AI-specific, recent work.**
Every expert has produced something in 2025 or 2026 specifically about AI content or AI search — not retrofitted "AI" branding on old SEO advice.

**3. Their perspectives create productive tension.**
Ryan Law (74% of AI content is junk, editorial discipline first) vs. Tomasz Niezgoda (one-click AI workflows at 150k users) vs. Koray Tugberk GUBUR (semantic AI agents for architecture) vs. Lily Ray (GEO and LLM citation tracking) — these are genuinely different operating models. That tension is where the playbook's insights will come from.

**Intentionally excluded:**
- Generic "top 10 AI SEO tools" listicle authors
- Agency landing pages masquerading as thought leadership
- People who talk about AI SEO without documented production results
- Neil Patel, HubSpot, etc. — too broad and agency-promotional, not practitioner-specific
